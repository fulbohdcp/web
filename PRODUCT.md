# PRODUCT.md — HDCP

> Brand name is provisional ("HDCP" / "handicap"). Like a golf handicap, but for footballers.

## Product purpose

A social network for amateur footballers. Every player answers a quiz about how they
play and gets a collectible Panini-style card (a "figurita") with a real, verifiable
score. The card is the trophy; logging matches week to week is the habit.

Two engines:
1. **Identity** — your figurita: a number that says how you actually play, validated by
   the people you play with (minimum 5 blind validations), not your own ego.
2. **Retention** — log every match (amistoso / torneo amateur / torneo pro), tag who you
   played with, and the whole team rates each other. Tagged players get invited in. The
   social graph grows match by match.

Supports Fútbol 5, 7, 8, 9 and 11. One profile per player; the format is a tag on each
match, not a separate card.

## Register

- `register: mixed`
- **Landing / marketing surfaces → brand.** The design IS the pitch. Bold, loud, stadium energy.
- **App surfaces (onboarding, figurita, feed, match logging) → product.** Design serves the flow.

## Users

Amateur footballers, mostly 16–50, who play weekend matches with friends, in amateur
leagues, or played competitive/youth football. They love the banter ("who actually plays
well?"), they're competitive, and they live on WhatsApp and Instagram. Latin America first
(Spanish-language, Argentine football slang in copy), mobile-first.

## Tone

Confident, funny, a little provocative. The voice of the locker room: it teases the guy who
rates himself a 9 and can't hit the target. Honest over polite. Spanish (rioplatense
flavor), never corporate. Humor is part of the product, not decoration.

Examples that fit: "Dejá de decir que jugás bien. Ahora podés saberlo." / "Ya pensó 3
jugadas antes que vos." / "Ya en el primer tiempo estás pensando en el asado."

## Strategic principles

- **The number doesn't lie.** Self-evaluation is only a claim until peers validate it blind.
  Unverified cards look visibly unverified.
- **The card is shareable.** Every figurita is built to screenshot and drop in a group chat.
- **Comparison with humor.** Rankings by generation, gender and category. You vs your friends.
- **Come back to log.** The weekly hook is loading matches and tagging teammates, which pulls
  new players in.
- **Mobile-first, thumb-first.** One question per screen in onboarding. Big tap targets.

## Anti-references

- Not a corporate SaaS dashboard. No cream backgrounds, no soft rounded "friendly fintech".
- Not EA Sports FC / FIFA Ultimate Team literal cloning (inspiration, not imitation).
- Not a stats-spreadsheet tool. The emotion (collectible, bragging rights) leads; the data backs it.
- Not generic "sports app neon-on-black". Stadium-night palette is warm and editorial, not gamer-RGB.
